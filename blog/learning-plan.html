My learning plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My long-term goal is to find a fulfilling career in the tech industry. Currently, I aim to be a web developer hence why I am attending Dev Academy. Initially, I want to get a job as a junior developer to learn as much as possible from my peers, and then I will see what next steps make sense for my career progression. My background is in the healthcare industry, so I would love to find a job at the intersection between healthcare and technology. However, I am open to pursuing a career in whatever tech speciality feels right as long as the position challenges me and provides room for continuous growth.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

Strengths: I am hard-working and persistent and will do what it takes to learn new information and meet my goals. I have strong research skills and am good at using the internet to find answers. I have experience with distance learning, which will be helpful when attending the online cohort of Dev Academy.

Weaknesses: I find it easy to get stuck down a rabbit hole and spend too long working on something relatively insignificant. I also avoid asking for help and instead try to find the answers to a problem myself, which is usually inefficient. Additionally, I sometimes avoid challenges in favour of sticking to what I'm good at. I am trying to adopt a new mindset where I actively seek out challenges and embrace them with an attitude that challenges are amazing for personal growth.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I want to develop my leadership and collaboration skills which I will do by participating in peer coding and group projects. I want to gain self-confidence in my abilities and decisions. I want to become a strong problem solver who embraces new challenges instead of sticking to my comfort zone.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme

I will keep a strict study schedule to ensure that I am staying caught up in my coursework. I will be efficient with my time by using Toggl to manage my time on individual tasks. I will maintain effective communication with my peers and facilitators to ensure. I will keep a habit of tracking my goals and to-do list. I will embrace a growth mindset and remind myself that to grow, I need to embrace challenges. This will help me remain mentally resilient when the course becomes more difficult. I will also prioritize looking after my physical health, such as getting enough sleep, eating well and exercising. I will also seek emotional support from my family and friends as I need it.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

When I am stuck, I will first try to find a solution independently e.g. By thoroughly proofreading my code. If this doesn't work, I will use Google and Discord to see if other students have had a similar problem. If I still haven't found a solution, I will contact my peers and facilitators via Discord. During this, I will try my best to describe the issue and describe what I have already tried to do to fix it.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

I expect that the facilitators will give me regular insightful feedback on what I am doing well and areas for improvement. I want to feel supported by the facilitation team and know they will be available to help me when I need it (during reasonable work hours). I also expect them to teach me industry best practices and the essential skills of a great programmer.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things.

I am fortunate enough to have minimal commitments and no dependents. However, I want to continue taking the time to do things that keep my mental and physical well-being in check. I will block out time for exercise each morning before 8:30 am. I will also dedicate a few hours on the weekend to connect with loved ones or do an activity that revitalizes me.
