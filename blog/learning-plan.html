My learning plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My long term goal is to find a fulfilling career in the tech industry. Currently I aim to be a web developer hence why I am attending Dev Academy. Initially I want to get a job as a junior developer so that I can learn as much as possible from my peers and then I will what next steps make sense for my career progression. My background is in the healthcare industry, so I would love to find a job in the intersection between healthcare and technology. However, I am open to pursuing a career in whatever tech specialty feels right as long as I find a position that challenges me and provides room for continuous growth.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

Strengths: I am hard-working and persistent and will do what it takes to learn new information and meet my goals. I have strong research skills and am good at using the internet to find answers. I have experience with distance learning which will be useful when attending the online cohort of Dev Academy. Weaknesses: I find it easy to get stuck down a rabbit hole and spend too long working on something relatively insignificant. I also avoid asking for help and instead try and find the answers to a problem myself but this is often inefficient. Additionally I sometimes avoid challenges in favour of sticking to what I'm good at. I am trying to adopt a new mindset where I actively seek out challenges and embrace them with a mindset that challenges are good for personal growth.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I want to develop my leadership and collaboration skills which I will do by participating in peer coding and group projects. I want to gain self-confidence in my abilities and decisions. I want to become a strong problem solver who embraces new challenges instead of sticking to my comfort zone.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme

I will keep to a strict study schedule to ensure that I am not falling behind in my coursework. I will be efficient with my time by using Toggl to manage the time I spend on individuals tasks. I will maintain effective communication with my peers and facilitators to ensure . I will maintain a habit of tracking my goals and to do list I will embrace a growth mindset remind myself that in order to grow I need to embrace challenges. This will help me to remain mentally resilient when the course is becoming more difficult. I will also prioritize looking after my physical health such as getting enough sleep, eating well and exercising. I will seek emotional support from my family and friend

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

When I am stuck I will first try to find a solution independently e.g. by thoroughly proofreading my code. If this doesn’t work then I will use google and check Discord to see if other students have had a similar problem. If I still haven't found a solution then I will reach out to my peers and facilitators via discord. During which I will try my best to describe the issue and describe what I have already tried to do to fix it.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

I expect that the facilitators will give me regular insightful feedback on what I am doing well and areas for improvement. I want to feel supported by the facilitation team and know that they will be available to help me when I need it (during reasonable work hours). I also expect them to teach me industry best practices and the most important skills of a great programmer.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things.

I am fortunate enough to have minimal commitments and no dependents. However, I want to continue taking the time to do things that keep my mental and physical wellbeing in check. I will block out time for exercise each morning before 8:30am. I will also dedicate a few hours on the weekend to connect with loved ones and/or do an activity that revitalizes myself.
